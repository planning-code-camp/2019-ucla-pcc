Join us for the first Planning Code Camp, a 5-week bootcamp and learn software tools for analyzing large data in planning including R, Dataviz, tidyverse, bash, dyplr, Git, and Github. Workshop runs from January - February (Week 3 - Week 8). We will serve dinner each session and have planning professionals give lightning talks at the start and end of the bootcamp. We will end with a hackathon that allows students to apply their newly learned skills to address a local civic issue. Although we encourage registrants to attend every session, you are welcome to attend individual workshop sessions if you already have knowledge of particular skills. When registering, please indicate if you are planning on skipping any sessions, so we have accurate counts for each session. Participants will be encouraged to help one another and to apply what they have learned to their own research problems.
